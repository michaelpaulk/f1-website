2025 Formula 1 Teams and Drivers

Each team fields two drivers. Modern teams operate like advanced engineering companies, with hundreds of staff developing aerodynamics, power units, and software tools. Below is the current lineup for the 2025 season.

Red Bull Racing

  • Max Verstappen
  • Sergio Pérez

Red Bull continues to focus on aerodynamic efficiency and strategic race pace.

Ferrari

  • Charles Leclerc
  • Carlos Sainz

Ferrari’s pursuit of stability and stronger development cycles has made them title contenders again.

Mercedes

  • Lewis Hamilton
  • George Russell

Mercedes remains a benchmark for hybrid power efficiency and strategic consistency.

McLaren

  • Lando Norris
  • Oscar Piastri

McLaren's technical revival is driven by aggressive mid-season upgrades and strong driver pairing.

Aston Martin

  • Fernando Alonso
  • Lance Stroll

Focused on aero gains and front-end stability.

Alpine

  • Pierre Gasly
  • Esteban Ocon

Aiming to refine power delivery and balance performance across circuits.

Williams

  • Alex Albon
  • Logan Sargeant

Long-term rebuild focusing on infrastructure and driver development.

RB (Visa Cash App)

  • Daniel Ricciardo
  • Yuki Tsunoda

Close technical partnership with Red Bull provides shared aero concepts.

Sauber

  • Valtteri Bottas
  • Zhou Guanyu

Preparing for the Audi works transition.

Haas

  • Nico Hülkenberg
  • Kevin Magnussen

Simplified car philosophy, incremental upgrades during season.
