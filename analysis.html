Las Vegas Grand Prix – Overview

The Las Vegas Grand Prix delivered the usual cocktail of street circuit unpredictability: cold temperatures, long straights, and heavy reliance on tire warm up. McLaren entered the weekend with a competitive package, but the way the race unfolded left them exposed at all the wrong times. Instead of showcasing their long run pace, they spent most of the event reacting to circumstances outside their control.
